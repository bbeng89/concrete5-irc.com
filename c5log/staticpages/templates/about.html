About concrete5-irc.com

Concrete5 is a free and open source content management system written in PHP. For more information on Concrete5 visit concrete5.org

This website is keeping a log of all the messages in the #concrete5 IRC channel on the Freenode network. The #concrete5 channel is a great place to discuss Concrete5 with other users and contains valuable information that may not be documented elsewhere. By logging the messages in #Concrete5 I hope to keep a history of this information so that it may help other Concrete5 users.

For comments or suggestions please email me at admin@concrete5-irc.com
